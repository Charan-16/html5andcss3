How Many Should We Put You Down For?

Posted by Brian on October 1st, 2013 at 2:39PM

The first big rule in sales is that if the person leaves empty-handed, they're likely not going to come back.

"Never give someone a chance to say no when selling your product."

That's why you have to be somewhat aggressive when you're working with a customer, but you have to make sure you don't overdo it and scare them away.
